How to play

To survive the deadly storm use the cursor to avoid the bouncing meteors and stay withing the yellow borders to maintain the ships course for eternal glory and intergalactic peace.

Meteorstorm IV

"AW SHIT NOW WHAT?"


The brave astronauts of the space ship Borealis are on a mission for FJASA. Their mission is to find intelligent life in space, and they are sure they have succeeded when they receive a signal from a planet not far away.


After 3 years in space on this seemingly hopeless quest, things are finally looking brighter. The crew start their journey towards the strange new planet, filled with new hope, as victory is closer than ever. Their joyfull cheers quickly die and their hopes and dreams are shattered when they see the danger ahead of them. They're stuck in a meteorstorm.


Will the astronauts ever make it out of the storm alive, or have they lost all hope of a happy ending? Their fates truely sealed, and doomed for the rest of all eternity. Will the crushed remains of the once so proud and great ship Borealis float around in the pityfull vacuum of space? You are their last hope. Help the astronauts of the Borealis out of this devastating hell.
